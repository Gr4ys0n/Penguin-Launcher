Lax1dude

Lax1dude is the original creator of eaglercraft. He ported many versions of eaglercraft to TeamVM including 1.5.2 and 1.8.8. He pushed the limits for the game, and ultimately he is the reason why eaglercraft is here today.

Ayunami2000

Ayunami2000 created eaglercraft alongside Lax1dude. He is resposible for many bug fixes, resource pack compatibility, and other small, yet important parts of the game.

Peyton Playz

Peyton Playz created both the eaglercraft versions, beta 1.7.3 and 1.12. He had to port 1.12 to EaglercraftX engine and TeamVM, also he made many bug/performance fixes for the version.
